Website Handoff Guide

Prepared by Adam Badran  ·  April 2026

Overview

Your website is complete and live. This guide walks you through three things: setting up your hosting account, connecting your own domain name, and receiving contact form submissions by email. The entire process takes about 20 minutes.

Step 1 — Create a Netlify Account

1.1

Go to netlify.com and sign up

Visit netlify.com and click Sign up. Create a free account using your email address. The free plan covers everything your site needs.

1.2

Send your account email to your developer

Once your account is created, send your Netlify account email address to Adam. He will initiate the site transfer to your account.

Step 2 — Accept the Site Transfer

2.1

Check your email for a transfer invitation

Netlify will send you an email inviting you to accept the site transfer. Click the link in that email.

2.2

Confirm the transfer in your dashboard

After clicking the link, log into your Netlify dashboard. The site drjangbakery should now appear under your sites. Your website is now fully under your account.

Step 3 — Buy and Connect Your Domain

3.1

Open your site in the Netlify dashboard

Click on your site, then go to Domain management in the left sidebar.

3.2

Add a custom domain

Click Add a domain and type the domain name you want (for example, drjangfactory.com). If it is available to purchase, Netlify will offer to register it for you directly. The cost is approximately $15 per year.

If the domain you want is not available as a .com, common alternatives for Korean businesses include .co.kr or .kr, which are registered through Korean registrars separately.
3.3

Complete the purchase

Enter your payment details to complete the domain purchase. Netlify will automatically connect the domain to your website — no additional technical steps are required. Allow up to 24 hours for the domain to become active worldwide, though it is usually much faster.

Step 4 — Set Up Email Notifications for the Contact Form

4.1

Go to the Forms section

In your Netlify dashboard, click Forms in the top navigation. You will see a form named contact — this is your website's enquiry form.

4.2

Add an email notification

Click Form notifications → Add notification → Email notification. Enter the email address where you want to receive enquiries. Click Save.

From this point on, every time a visitor submits the contact form on your website, you will receive a full email with their name, company, phone number, and message.

Viewing Past Submissions

All form submissions are also stored in your Netlify dashboard under Forms → contact. You can view every enquiry there, download them as a spreadsheet (CSV), or delete old ones. Your free plan stores up to 100 submissions per month.

The website's code is maintained by your developer. For any changes — text updates, new product photos, design adjustments — contact Adam directly. Updates are typically deployed within 24 hours and go live automatically.
